Guide to Maximize Workout Results


Workout Tips

  1. Go into your workouts with a purpose and a goal in mind
  2. Chart the length of time it takes to complete your workout each time you go out. Use this as a measure of progress and a means to increase efficiency
  3. Add on an extra rep/set each week. This will ensure that you are getting better with every passing week
  4. Try to workout at the same time every day. This will help you maintain a schedule and build up a habit for working out
  5. Take at least 2 days off every week to allow your body to properly recover
  6. Be sure to log your workouts in order to keep track of your progress

Lifestyle Tips

  1. Keep a set sleep schedule. Go to bed at the same time everyday and ensure that you get the recommended 8 hours. SLEEP IS MOST CRUCIAL IN RECOVERY AND RESULTS!
  2. Keep a strict diet plan to ensure you are not eating away your gains
  3. Try to keep the stress in your life to a minimum. Stress can cloud your mind and can be detrimental to workouts in terms of motivation and effectiveness
  4. Develop strong time management skills to ensure you give yourself enough time to workout and complete the rest of your daily tasks
  5. Never give up! No matter how slowly you think you are progressing, remember that there still is progress. Keep the end goal in sight.
  6. Try using your smartphone to enhance and improve your sleep quality. We recommend trying the app Sleep Cycle on the App Store.




Diet Tips

  1. Have protein with every meal (about 2 fists worth)
  2. Have 2 cups of fruit/vegetables with every meal
  3. 8 cups of water per day
  4. Eliminate (limit) sugary and diet drinks
  5. Eat 3 meals a day, especially breakfast
  6. Get plenty of calcium
  7. Make sure you have a balanced plate with every meal
